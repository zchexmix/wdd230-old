    • Discover Events and Activities!

    • Bountiful has countless hiking trails that can be explored and even created! Roam the beautiful surrounding mountains that surround the area!
    • Bountiful is great for a nice walk along Bountiful Boulevard, you will be able to walk by the Bountiful LDS Temple, The Bountiful Cemetary, and get amazing views of the valley along the way!
    • Check out Main Street for loads of activities, shopping, and great food! Blacksmith Ice cream is a must have!
    • The South Davis Recreation center has every activity you could want for a day out with the family! Ice Skating, Swimming, Water slides, Basketball and raquetball, rock climbinh and a fully equiped gym with a running track! It has it all!
    • The Eaglewood Fireworks show every year at Eaglewood Golf course is amazing. Every July there are food trucks, Games, a car show, and amazing live music makes for a great night out!
    • Golfing here around Bountiful is great. Challenging courses and incredible views of the mountains will be fun for anyone thast loves to golf!
